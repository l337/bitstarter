Ever waited to long at a bar to order a drink? Was it ever too crowded in a establishment to even hear your waitress/waiter? And of course at the end of ordering and eating we are still stuck waiting around for the check. We are so far into technology, that things we would never thought of doing happens in a matter of seconds, why do we have to worry about these basic transactions that should only take a seconds to occure. We all have smart phones, why not take advantage of that and use it for everyday use. Sure we already do that but are we thinking of all the ways we can harness this capability?

I am impossing on NFC (Near field communitcation) enabled establishment. Where the table tops are enabled to recieve radio communication. Costumers are told to go to the establishment's web site. Upon going there it looks and feels like a native app, but it's just a menu of drinks and food items that the restaurant/bar has to offer. The food and drink items will vary upon location (for instance if I am looking for a beer that is only sold in a few places then there needs to be some modifications for the business) so the items will be controlled through a simple custom CMS interface that looks and feels like the business (i.e. follows their style guide if they have one). The resturant employees will insert or remove items for instace if it's a seasonal item whether it is a beverage or a food item, the owner/employee of that estashment will have real time control over what gets displayed on the menu. This will also save on printing new paper, or discouraging costumers when an item is sold out. When the costumer loads the site (i.e. http://www.yourbusiness.com/nfc-menu) that they will get to buy tapping counter-top in the restaurant, s/he will have a tray (analogis to a shopping cart) they will be able to add as much items as they want. When they are finished they will simply tap the phone either on the table they're sitting at or at the bar (via NFC api for web, for more information visit: http://www.w3.org/wiki/Further_background_on_NFC). As soon as that happens the bar/resturant will be notified and they will be placed in a queue with their name. People will have the opportunity to share their meals with others by tapping their phone with another person's phone.
